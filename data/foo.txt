<h1 class="entry-title">Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup</h1>
<div class="post-info"><span class="date published time" title="2017-01-23T06:00:21+00:00">January 23, 2017</span> By <span class="author vcard"><span class="fn"><a href="http://www.afamilyfeast.com/author/martha/" rel="author">Martha</a></span></span> <span class="post-comments"><a href="http://www.afamilyfeast.com/citrus-chicken-and-rice-soup/#comments">2 Comments</a></span> </div><div class="entry-content"><div class='shareaholic-canvas' data-app-id='7683774' data-app='share_buttons' data-title='Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup' data-link='http://www.afamilyfeast.com/citrus-chicken-and-rice-soup/' data-summary=''></div><p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-21809 size-full" src="http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup.jpg" alt="Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup - Orange, lemon and lime juice add a fresh flavor twist to this classic chicken soup recipe." width="700" height="1050" srcset="http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup.jpg 700w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup-200x300.jpg 200w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup-683x1024.jpg 683w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px"/></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><em>Looking for a new twist on a classic chicken soup recipe? Today’s Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup is for you!</em></p>
<p>I’m someone who loves a bowl of warm, comforting chicken soup just about any day of the week.  This easy and delicious Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup is destined to become a new favorite!</p>
<p>We started with classic chicken and rice soup – but added orange juice to the broth, plus a little lemon and lime juice too.  The fresh citrus juices add a wonderful brightness to the soup – reminiscent of an Asian-inspired broth, and fresh cilantro and chopped scallions on top are the perfect finishing touch.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-21810 size-full" src="http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup3.jpg" alt="Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup - Orange, lemon and lime juice add a fresh flavor twist to this classic chicken soup recipe." width="700" height="1050" srcset="http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup3.jpg 700w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup3-200x300.jpg 200w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup3-683x1024.jpg 683w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup3-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px"/></p>
<p>Inspiration for this Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup recipe came from a <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=air.com.yudu.ReaderAIR6194362&amp;hl=en" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">free recipe magazine from our local supermarket</a> – but we made it our own using <a href="http://www.afamilyfeast.com/chicken-stock/" target="_blank">Homemade Chicken Stock</a> and <a href="http://www.afamilyfeast.com/sunday-cooking-lesson-perfect-poached-chicken/" target="_blank">Perfect Poached Chicken</a>, plus some other changes. To make the recipe prep even easier, feel free to use a store-bought rotisserie chicken and canned chicken broth instead.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-21811 size-full" src="http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup2.jpg" alt="Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup - Orange, lemon and lime juice add a fresh flavor twist to this classic chicken soup recipe." width="700" height="1050" srcset="http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup2.jpg 700w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup2-200x300.jpg 200w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup2-683x1024.jpg 683w, http://cdn1.afamilyfeast.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/Citrus-Chicken-Soup2-300x450.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px"/></p>
<p>This Citrus Chicken and Rice Soup is a wonderful way to cook with in-season citrus fruits, and it’s a delicious way to warm up your belly this winter!<br/>
